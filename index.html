Our Sacred Vision

New Earth Communities

Creating regenerative living spaces that model sustainable relationships with the land and each other.

Ancient Wisdom

Global Expansion

As an extension of LuminaNova.org, we bridge ancient wisdom with contemporary sustainability practices.

Pod Communities

Self-sustaining living spaces designed for those seeking to reconnect with Gaia and live in harmony with natural cycles.

Mesa Verde Pods

Located in the shadow of Mesa Verde National Park, these pods honor the cliff-dwelling peoples who called this land home.

  • Solar-powered living spaces
  • Permaculture gardens
  • Community gathering spaces
  • Traditional craft workshops

Ute Mountain Pods

Nestled in the sacred Ute Mountain region, offering deep connection to indigenous wisdom and mountain energy.

  • Water harvesting systems
  • Natural building techniques
  • Meditation and ceremony spaces
  • Herbal medicine gardens

Ancient Wisdom & Land Stewardship

We offer educational programs that bridge indigenous knowledge with contemporary sustainability practices, honoring the wisdom keepers who have stewarded this land for generations.

Traditional Agriculture

Learn ancestral farming methods including three sisters planting, water-wise cultivation, and soil regeneration.

Sacred Ecology

Understand the spiritual dimensions of land relationship and ceremony-based stewardship practices.

Natural Building

Master techniques using local materials: adobe, cob, straw bale, and earth-friendly construction methods.

Water Wisdom

Sacred relationship with water through harvesting, conservation, and ceremony in this high desert environment.
